Objective

Is there a single thing that directly connects Confucius to Juliette Binoche? Who knows?! But there is a route from Alexander the Great to Pink Floyd through just 3 other things ( 4 links between them ). Depends on how much you know about all the uses humans make of horses.

Pick a challenge from the menu and let your imagination and lateral thinking wander.

Clik the button with the link icon between two phrases to ask the internet if the Wikipedia article for the thing on the top has a direct link to one at the bottom. The answer, which might take 2-3 seconds, is indicated by a green tick or a red cross. If you type in another word, the button is reset again.

Happy hopping!
